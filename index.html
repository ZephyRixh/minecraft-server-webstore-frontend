Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I join on Java Edition?

Add the server IP play.eclipxmc.fun to your Minecraft server list. EclipX MC supports cross-version connectivity, allowing you to join from any Minecraft release without compatibility issues.

How do I join on Bedrock Edition?

Use the same IP play.eclipxmc.fun on Bedrock Edition. The port is 19132 (default). Works on console, mobile, and Windows 10/11.

Is the server free to play?

Yes! EclipX MC is completely free to join and play. The store offers optional ranks and perks that enhance your experience but are never required to enjoy the server.

How do I receive my rewards after voting?

After voting on any of the voting sites listed above, you will automatically receive your rewards in-game. You can vote once every 24 hours per site for maximum rewards.

How do I purchase packages?

Browse our store, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll be redirected to our Discord server where our staff will assist you with payment and delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal and UPI as payment methods, with transactions processed in INR and USD. All payments are handled securely through our Discord support system.
